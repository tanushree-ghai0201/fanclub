About Us

Hello potter heads! To all the no-majs and muggles, witches and wizards’ there are hundreds of potter heads lurking all over Delhi and it’s about time that we come together and embrace our obsession over harry potter and the wizarding world.
Welcome to Delhi’s biggest harry potter fan club. We are a group of undercover wizards amongst muggles. We are a wide group of people spreading all around the Delhi- NCR area with about a five hundred group members now. And we invite all the Gryffindors, Hufflepuffs, Slytherins and Ravenclaws out there to join our community of wizards and witches. We are a group of people bounded by our common love of the wizarding world.

Events

We have a wide range of events and activities that are as diverse as the interests of our members. We strive to provide an experience that allows you to indulge in your talents and creativity while having a tremendously fun time.
Whether you can ace the toughest harry potter trivia or are new to the fantasy altogether, all are welcome!
We meet first Saturday of every month and do a lot of fun activities and revel our muggle-dom which include:
 Trivia
 Cosplay events
 Hands at harry potter DIY
 Harry potter themed board games
 Analyzing the harry potter series very deeply
 Role plays
 Harry potter theme parties
We also have special chief guests every week who gives out prizes to all the winners in the events which take place.

In short our group is a heaven for all potter heads out there.
We usually meet up at different harry potter themed cafes all over Delhi.
